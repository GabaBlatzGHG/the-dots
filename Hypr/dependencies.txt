waybar
mako
rofi
ghostty
dolphin
firefox
brightnessctl

swaybg

## Copy/paste install command (official repos only)
sudo pacman -S --needed hyprland hyprpaper waybar mako rofi ghostty dolphin firefox brightnessctl pamixer flameshot openrgb swaybg dbus gnome-settings-daemon qt6ct kvantum networkmanager network-manager-applet wireplumber playerctl
